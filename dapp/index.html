zFi is a meta DEX aggregator. Every quote races 10+ sources in parallel and picks the best price automatically — or you can choose manually.

External aggregators: 0x, ParaSwap, KyberSwap, 1inch, Odos, OKX, CoW Protocol, Bebop, Enso, OpenOcean (Bitget coming soon).

On-chain quoter (zQuoter): Unlike off-chain APIs, zQuoter discovers routes directly on the blockchain via Uniswap V2/V3/V4, SushiSwap, Curve, zAMM, and Lido — with atomic multi-hop, split routing, and per-leg slippage protection. No solvers, no servers, no API keys. Works even when every external API is down.

Zero protocol fees. No intermediaries. Your trade goes directly from your wallet to the pools.

Launch a Coin

ez — memecoin-style launch. 80% of ETH raised seeds liquidity, 20% goes to a ragequitable treasury. No ops allocation, no vesting, no tap. Buyers can always exit at the treasury-backed floor price. Best for community tokens, memes, and experiments.

fund — structured fundraise (DAICO). Includes an ops allocation with lockup, tap vesting that drips treasury ETH to ops over time, and full governance. Buyers fund a project and the DAO controls how funds are spent. Best for projects, protocols, and teams that need operating capital.

collect — collector DAO. Pool ETH to mint NFTs or place open bids for tokens. Bare Moloch with ragequitable vault. Share sale via vault.buy(). Best for group buys, mint pools, and bounties.
